The perfect place to enjoy your coffee

Coffee Spot was founded on September 6, 2023, with the goal of providing a welcoming environment for coffee lovers. We are proud in offering a pleasant coffee experience unlike any other in the city.

At Coffee Spot, we believe that great food and drinks go together. Whether you’re craving hot or iced coffee, a fresh pastry, a comforting pasta dish, or a hearty rice meal, we have something for everyone.

From morning coffee to a late lunch, Coffee Spot is your go-to destination for a wide variety of meals, all served in a relaxing and cozy atmosphere.
